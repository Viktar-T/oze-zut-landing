# 6.1 Engine_bench-R121

Engine testing and research bench for performance analysis and optimization.

**Location:** Room  
**Type:** Engine Testing Equipment  
**Configuration:** Research Bench

## Access

This engine testing bench is designed for comprehensive performance testing, efficiency analysis, and optimization research of various engine configurations. The system enables detailed monitoring of engine parameters, emissions, and performance characteristics.

## Purpose

The engine bench is used for:
- Performance testing of various engine configurations
- Efficiency analysis and optimization
- Emissions monitoring and analysis
- Fuel consumption studies
- Research and development of engine optimization strategies
- Educational demonstrations

## Features

- Comprehensive engine testing capabilities
- Real-time parameter monitoring
- Emissions measurement equipment
- Performance data collection
- Educational and research applications
